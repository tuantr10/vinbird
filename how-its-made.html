Making Bird's Nest

We provide hygienic and safe products to consumer by setting the highest standard of manufacturing.

Step 01

Nest sorting

  • We receive the raw bird's nest from Swiftlet farm.
  • We do initial sorting based on its shape, feather, and color.
  • We store the bird's nest in a cold room after being sorted.

Step 02

Cleansing

  • We cleanse the bird's nest in flowing water to remove the physical dirt.
  • Cleansing also helps to soften the nest for the next process.

Step 03

Feathers Picking

  • Our trained staff handpick the feather residue one-by-one to ensure no feather left behind.
  • This process requires patient and precision to get the best outcome.

Step 04

Finishing

We refine the shape of bird's nest that may get bruised during the feathers picking process.

Step 05

Grading

  • Our trained staff handpick the feather residue one-by-one to ensure no feather left behind.
  • This process requires patient and precision to get the best outcome.

Step 06

Heat Treatment

The nests which are already graded are steamed in this room. The minimal core of the nests should be 70 degrees Celcius for at least 3,5 seconds. The heat treatment will eliminate bacteria, germs, and avian influenza virus.

Step 07

Packing

We cover the bird’s nest using a vacuum packaging that guarantee its hygiene.
